Responsive Cat Rescue

About Our Cat Rescue

We rescue cats of all ages, breeds, and healths. Our goal is to place each cat with suitable foster pet-parents until the perferct forever home is found. If cats come to us in pairs, we will only allow potential pet-parents to adopt them as a pair. We do not separate cat families and friends from one another. Each foster pet-parent and forever pet-parent is carefully vetted prior to being matched with any cats. We take each cat's safety and joy very seriously.

We have been rescuing and placing cats for over 25 years! Our volunteers and board members find this work to be incredibly fulfilling. There is nothing we'd rather do than save and take care of cats. If you know someone who is struggling to care for their favorite feline, please refer them to us. We will gladly help them care for their cat or help them rehome their furry friend.

Our Values

Love

We love each cat as if it were our own. We will never abandon any cat in our care and strive to do what's best for each feline, no matter the cost.

Joy

Every cat deserves to experience joy. We ensure each cat has the freedom to play or rest whenever suits them best.

Commitment

We are committed to our cats, volunteers, and employees. We begin each day by thinking about how we can make their lives better.

Our Cats

Mochi

Mochi is 3 years old and sweet as can be. She loves cuddling on the sofa and purring when she's happy. Mochi is great with other animals and by herself - she's not a picky kitty! If you take Mochi home with you, you'll surely fall in love with her.

Charlie

Charlie is a 1-year-old Scottish Fold. He can sit like a human and eat like one too. He loves mazes and mice. Charlie has a very sweet temperament and enjoys watching people bake. He's not the smartest cat, but he makes up for it with endless kindness.

Fluffanilla

Fluffanilla is the fluffiest cat in town. We don't know where the fluff ends and the cat begins. Luckily, all that fluff makes for some great soft cuddles as long as you're not allergic to pet hair. She loves a good belly rub.

Meowie

Meowie is a very vocal and clingy cat. He doesn't like to be left alone and he has no idea what it means to be quiet. He has a series of meows for every emotion. He also loves schedules, so if you don't feed him on time, you'll hear about it!

KnightRider

KnightRider is a very brave kitty! She will protect you no matter how large the threat. She also loves driving around in cars, and is a fan of sunglasses and leather jackets. KnightRider also has great hair and enjoys listening to electronic music.
